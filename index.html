Who I Am

About Me

Raised in Chicago, IL — the city that shaped my character and taught me how to push through anything. In 2019, I was involved in a life-changing hit and run accident that put me in a three-week coma and left me with permanent vision loss in my left eye.

"Since then, I've had one gear — forward. If anything, losing something made me hungrier to build something."

From car detailer to customization shop owner to automotive repair shop manager — the car scene has been woven into everything I do. Chicago raised me on it and I built a career out of it.

But before the cars, there was the controller. Competed in Fortnite back when it first dropped, and computers have always been in my world. When GTA 6 drops — I'll be live. Follow me and stay tuned.
